About Wasp

The Wasp System is an integrated LIMS and batch processing system.

Integrated

The Wasp System integrates LIMS functionality, sample management, job submission, and automated analytical workflows for massively parallel sequencing.

Powerful

Integrate the Wasp System with your local high performace computing system. Configure more as your needs grow.

Extensible

The Wasp System is designed from its core to be extensible. Develop plugins for workflows, software, and assays.
